    Rolex is a renowned Swiss watch brand and manufacturer, headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland. Founded in 1905 by Hans Wilsdorf and Alfred Davis in London, the company initially operated under the name "Wilsdorf and Davis" before registering the "Rolex" brand name in 1908. After World War I, the company moved its base of operations to Geneva because of the unfavorable economy in the United Kingdom. In 1920, Hans Wilsdorf registered Montres Rolex SA in Geneva as the new company name (montre is French for watch); it later became Rolex SA.
